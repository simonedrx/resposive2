ABOUT

Simone Droga is a visionary artist in the field of 3D art, renowned for their captivating and thought-provoking creations. With an innate ability to push boundaries and challenge conventions, Simone has carved a distinct path in the art world.

Their artworks delve into realms of imagination and perception, exploring deep and complex themes. Through their creations, Simone seeks to capture and express the essence of human emotions, creating a dialogue between the viewer's mind and heart.

Their art is characterized by a unique combination of intricate details and surreal elements, blending together to create visually extraordinary compositions. Their works range from representations of fantastical and futuristic worlds to portraits that reveal the intimate complexity of the human psyche.

Simone is also known for their technique and their ability to experiment with different digital tools and techniques. Through their prowess in the 3D realm, they bring to life scenarios and characters that seem to originate from another dimension, inviting the viewer to immerse themselves in a surreal universe.

Always in pursuit of new forms of artistic expression, Simone has collaborated with other figures in the art world, opening new perspectives and pushing the boundaries of their art even further. Their passion and dedication have led them to collaborate with fellow artists, transforming their work into a bridge between different creative spheres.

Simone Droga is an artist who continues to explore the infinite range of human emotions through their art. Currently residing in Italy, they continue to create new works for public exhibitions and select clients. Their ability to capture the imagination and evoke deep emotions makes Simone an extraordinary artist, capable of captivating and engaging audiences with their unique and provocative style.
